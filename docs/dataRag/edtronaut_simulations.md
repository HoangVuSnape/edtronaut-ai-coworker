# The AI Co-Worker Engine Strategy

## Current Capabilities
Edtronaut currently utilizes an **AI Assessment Engine** to analyze and evaluate learner submissions. This evaluation mechanism provides:
- Rubric-based scoring.
- Comprehensive narrative feedback (e.g., overall score, criteria breakdown).
- Constructive insights structured as "What you did well / What to improve".

## The Next Evolution: AI Co-Workers
The strategic next step is the deployment of the **AI Co-Worker Engine**. This engine aims to enable Simulation Takers (Users) to organically work, talk, and collaborate with AI Stakeholders inside the simulation.

### AI Engine Concept
- **Not Just "Chatbots"**: These agents go beyond answering questions; they act with distinct personalities, persist memory across the conversation, and drive specific business functions and goals.
- **NPC Collaboration**: Designed similarly to NPCs (Non-Player Characters) in video games but tailored for the professional workplace (e.g., A demanding Marketing Director, a skeptical Data Scientist, or a friendly but busy Project Manager).
- **The Supervisor Layer**: A 'Director' agent works invisibly alongside the characters to monitor the pacing of the simulation, ensuring the user stays on track and providing subtle hints if the conversation goes in circles.
