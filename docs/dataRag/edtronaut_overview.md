# Edtronaut Company Overview

## About Edtronaut
**Edtronaut** is an innovative EdTech and HRTech startup redefining workforce readiness through an AI-enabled Job Simulation Platform.

### Key Mission
The core mission of Edtronaut is to mirror real-world job tasks within simulations, enabling learners to gain practical experience and develop job-ready skills.

### Core Features
1. **Hands-on Experience**: Learners are immersed in real-world scenarios, solving problems through direct interactions with dynamic AI co-workers, stakeholders, and simulated environments.
2. **Tangible Outcomes**: Users build a verifiable portfolio of simulations that can be shared and showcased to others, including potential employers and recruiters, effectively demonstrating their tangible skills.
3. **Social Sharing**: The platform makes it simple for users to share internally or externally across different social platforms like LinkedIn, Facebook, and Threads to boost their professional visibility.
