# Planning: 12-Month Talent & Leadership Strategy (Gucci Group)
**Author**: Gucci Group CEO / CHRO
**Confidentiality**: Internal Use Only

## Executive Summary
This document outlines our 12-month strategic roadmap for the "HRM Talent & Leadership Development" initiative across the Gucci Global Group. The primary objective is to align individual brand autonomy with our unified Group HR mission, focusing heavily on our newly established Competency Framework: **Vision, Entrepreneurship, Passion, and Trust**.

---

## Q1: The Diagnostics Phase
* **Objective**: Establish a baseline across all regional brands.
* **Key Actions**:
  * Roll out the standardized Competency Framework assessment tool.
  * *Employer Branding & IC Regional Manager* to conduct interviews with local branch heads to identify potential resistance to the new process.
  * Audit current inter-brand mobility metrics.

## Q2: Alignment & Regional Pilot
* **Objective**: Address regional skills gaps and pilot the mobility program.
* **Key Actions**:
  * Utilize AI-driven skill mapping to surface the top 10% of high-potential talent matching the "Entrepreneurship" and "Vision" competencies.
  * Initiate the cross-brand mentorship program in the APAC and EMEA regions.
  * Ensure that local Brand DNA remains supported—avoid imposing overly rigid corporate structures where creativity thrives.

## Q3: Full Rollout & Structural Integration
* **Objective**: Implement the framework universally and track progress.
* **Key Actions**:
  * Connect performance evaluations to the four core competencies.
  * *CHRO*: Host quarterly leadership alignment workshops with the Group CEO to review the balance between group needs and brand autonomy.
  * Utilize the "Simple A/B Simulator" tools to test alternative communication strategies for the new policy roll-out in historically resistant markets.

## Q4: Review & Scale
* **Objective**: Measure impact, gather feedback, and create tangible assets for the upcoming fiscal year.
* **Key Actions**:
  * Export the "Portfolio Pack" outlining the full plan, execution updates, and generated internal posts.
  * Publish the internal "Gucci Talent Mobility Report" to stakeholders.
  * Set adjusted targets for the following 12 months based on Q4 performance metrics against the Competency Framework.
